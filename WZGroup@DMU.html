WANG Group

Dr. Zhaogui Wang is a Lecturer at Dalian Maritime University. Dr. Wang's expertise crosses finite-element-based modeling with polymer deposition additive manufacturing with a special focus on short fiber filled thermoplastics. He has published over 15 peer-reviewed articles on international journals and conference proceeds, in which 6 are SCI indexed. He is the PI of a national foundation of science of China (NSFC) grant (CNY 240,000) and a major participant of another NSFC grant (CNY 580,000). In addition, he has participated in a ORNL-funded project (USD 100,000) in Baylor University. He is colaborating with prof. Dr. Douglas E. Smith at Baylor University in developing numerical analysis tools for large area polymer composites extrusion deposition additive manufacturing systems.

INTRO
The main focus of this group is additive manufacturing and advanced polymeric materials. Current interests include the additive manufacturing process optimization of high tempearture materials including PEEK, PEI and so forth; Integrating fused filament fabrication and post thermal treatment into a uniform additive manufacturing system; Additively manufacturing sandwiched structural functional graded materials with two different polymer materials; Developing finite-element-based simulation for thermal-mechanical characterization of the additive manufacturign process; Tailoring artifical intelligence (AI) algorithms to optimizing the process of extrusion deposition additive manufacturing; Material flow and fiber orientation prediction in short fiber filled polymer composites additive manufacturing.

王肇贵课题组

王肇贵博士是大连海事大学讲师。王博士的专业知识包括基于有限元的建模和聚合物沉积添加剂制造，特别关注短纤维填充热塑性塑料。他在国际期刊和会议上发表了超过15篇同行评议文章，其中6篇被SCI收录。他是中国国家自然科学基金会青年基金项目（24万元）的主持人和另一个面上项目（58万元)的主要参加者。此外，他还在贝勒大学参与了一个由美国橡树岭国家实验室（ORNL）资助的项目（10万美元）。他正在与贝勒大学的道格拉斯·E·史密斯教授合作，为大面积聚合物复合材料挤出沉积增材制造系统开发数值分析工具。

课题组介绍
该组的主要重点是增材制造和先进的聚合物材料。目前的兴趣包括PEEK，PEI等高温材料的增材制造工艺优化；将熔融长丝制造和后热处理集成到均匀的增材制造系统中；用两种不同的聚合物材料附加制造夹层结构功能梯度材料；开发基于有限元的增材制造过程热机械特性仿真；定制人工智能（AI）算法以优化挤出沉积增材制造过程；短纤维填充聚合物复合材料增材制造中的材料流动和纤维取向预测。
